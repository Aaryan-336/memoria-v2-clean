# Do Not Do

- Do not replace FastAPI.
- Do not replace React.
- Do not replace FAISS without approval.
- Do not introduce LangChain unless requested.
- Do not migrate databases without approval.
- Do not expose secrets.
- Do not commit API keys.
- Do not rewrite working modules.
- Do not delete migrations.
- Do not introduce paid infrastructure unnecessarily.
- Do not invent APIs or endpoints.
- Do not modify UI philosophy without updating design.md.
