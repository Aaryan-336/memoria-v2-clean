# Memoria Vision

## Mission

Transform lectures and educational content from any source into personalized learning experiences.

Memoria helps students convert lecture recordings, slides, PDFs, and notes into structured knowledge that is easier to understand, revise, and retain.

## Vision

Students spend countless hours revisiting lectures and manually creating notes.

Memoria aims to become an AI-powered lecture intelligence platform that automatically transforms raw educational content into:

- Personalized notes
- Topic-wise summaries
- Flashcards
- Revision material
- Semantic knowledge systems
- Interactive study assistance

## Success Metrics

Prototype:
- 100 active users.
- Process lecture material within 3 minutes.

Future:
- Subscription-based SaaS.
- Support 100,000+ students.
- Become a student's second brain for academics.
